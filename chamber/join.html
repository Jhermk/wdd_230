Membership Levels

Nonprofit

No fee

Plans in the Marketplace are presented in 4 business plan categories: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum.

Bronze

$100/month

Businesses that are for-profit and have less than 50 workers are eligible for bronze membership. Benefits comprise:

  • 30% discount on events
  • 30% discount For advertisement
  • 100% comission for referals

Silver

$150/month

For-profit companies with fewer than 250 workers are eligible for silver membership. Added advantages include:

  • 60% discount on events
  • 60% discount on advertising
  • 100% comission for referals
  • Free Transaction between members

Gold

$250/month

For-profit companies with more than 250 employees are eligible for gold membership. Additional benefits include:

  • 80% off tickets to events
  • Free attendance to the trade show
  • 100% comission for referals
  • 5 featured ads per year
  • Free Transaction between members
  • 1 free training per quarter
